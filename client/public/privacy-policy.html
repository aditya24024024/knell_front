Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Welcome to Knell ("we", "our", or "us"). Knell is a marketplace platform that connects buyers with sellers offering companion and freelance services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile app or website at knell.co.in.

By using Knell, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use Knell:

  • Account Information: Your full name, email address, phone number, and password when you register.
  • Profile Information: Your username, profile photo, and bio/description that you choose to add.
  • Gig Information: Service listings, descriptions, pricing, and images that sellers create.
  • Order Information: Details of transactions between buyers and sellers including order status and history.
  • Messages: Communications between users through our in-app messaging system.
  • Payment Information: Payment transactions are processed by Razorpay. We do not store your card or banking details directly.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and push notification tokens for sending you alerts.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, pages visited, and features used.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Facilitate transactions between buyers and sellers
  • Send you notifications about orders, messages, and account activity
  • Allow sellers to create and manage gig listings
  • Enable real-time messaging between users
  • Process payments securely through Razorpay
  • Improve and personalize your experience on the platform
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Detect and prevent fraud or misuse of the platform

3. Information We Share

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We share your data only in the following cases:

  • Razorpay: To process payments securely. Razorpay has its own privacy policy available at razorpay.com.
  • Cloudinary: Profile photos and gig images are stored on Cloudinary's servers.
  • Firebase: Used for sending push notifications to your device.
  • Other Users: Your public profile (name, username, profile photo, bio, and gigs) is visible to other users of the platform.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal information and associated data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

Messages and order history may be retained for up to 90 days after account deletion for dispute resolution purposes.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@knell.co.in.

6. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously. We implement the following measures to protect your information:

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS (SSL/TLS encryption)
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt before storage
  • Authentication uses JSON Web Tokens (JWT)
  • Sensitive data on your device is stored using encrypted secure storage

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

7. Children's Privacy

Knell is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we discover that a minor has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately. If you believe a minor has registered on our platform, please contact us at support@knell.co.in.

8. Push Notifications

With your permission, we send push notifications to keep you informed about messages, orders, and activity on the platform. You can disable push notifications at any time through your device settings.

9. Third-Party Links

Our app may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. Your continued use of Knell after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us:

  • Email: support@knell.co.in
  • Website: knell.co.in
